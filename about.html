About me

Hello and welcome to my blog! :)

My name is Annie. A few words about me.

Being curious by nature, I always enjoyed making trips to new places, discovering new horizons and learning how other people live. My life-long interest is nature of our world in all its aspects and guises. National Geographic and Around the World magazines were my monthly must-reads at university. When I started travelling by myself, it was like I dived into a whole new world. There were so many stories I brought back with me and wanted to share.

The first time a thought about a blog struck me in 2016 when I was staying at a house of my Korean host who had found me by Couchsurfing. Then I found out how many stereotypes about nations and countries reside in our heads. Somehow at some moment I began to feel as a representative who was responsible for the view of my country building up in people's minds during our encounters.

Eventually, that trip inspired me to research cultures, traditions, way of life, mentality of people I meet deeper and tell about it to the world. I really want people to be more conscious and respectful towards those keeping other customs. Understanding diminishes fear and plants seeds of amiability. To me, being broaded-minded means to be wiser.

Therefore, my mission is to break stereotypes and to show beauty of the world in diversity. Enjoy!
