About me

First time a thought about a blog struck me in 2016 when I was staying at a house of my Korean host who had found me by Couchsurfing. Then I found out how many stereotypes about nations and countries reside in our heads. Somehow at some moment I began to feel as a representative who was responsible for the view of my country building up in people's minds during our encounters.
